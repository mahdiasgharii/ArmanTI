# ArmanCTI

## What

ArmanCTI is a cyber threat intelligence platform built on OpenCTI. It provides security teams with a centralized workspace for managing threat actors, intrusion sets, malware, reports, and indicators — with dashboard analytics, relationship mapping, and AI-powered analysis features.

## Who

Security analysts, threat intelligence researchers, and CISO teams who need a professional, instrument-grade tool for tracking and analyzing cyber threats.

## Register

Product UI — dashboards, data tables, forms, and analytical tools. Design serves the product: clarity, density, and precision over decoration.

## Voice

Technical, precise, no-nonsense. The interface feels like a professional instrument, not a SaaS product. Lowercase typography as signature voice. Flat surfaces with tonal layering. Accent colors used surgically (≤15% of any screen).

## Anti-references

- Not OpenCTI with a paint job — the departure from default OpenCTI cyan/teal is intentional
- Not generic SaaS — no cookie-cutter card grids, hero-metric templates, or emoji icons
- Not "AI-generated aesthetic" — no purple gradients everywhere, glassmorphism, or "powered by AI" badges
- Not warm-neutral — black canvas, not cream/sand/beige
